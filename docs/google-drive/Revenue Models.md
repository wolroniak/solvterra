# **Goal**

Analysis of revenue streams and fit with business model.

---

# **Executive Summary**

**Best fit:**

* NGO contracts

* Municipal challenge programs

* EU/local grants

* University licensing

**Supporting:**

* CSR challenge sponsorships

* Brand reward partnerships

**Misaligned:**

* ESG/CSRD enterprise SaaS

Result: high mission alignment, short sales cycles, realistic technical load, scalable institutional model

---

# **Business Model: Key Characteristics**

**Value Proposition**

* Youth/student micro-volunteering

* Challenge-based sustainability actions

* Lightweight impact reporting

* Simple activity verification

**Customer Segments**

* Municipalities / public sector

* NGOs / civic organizations

* Universities / student unions

* CSR-oriented companies

* Sustainable consumer brands

**Capabilities**

* Digital activation platform

* Gamification and rewards

* Light impact verification

* Community engagement program design

* Fast setup, low integration

**Constraints**

* Small team

* Limited engineering capacity

* Need for short sales cycles

* No complex ESG compliance work

Overall orientation: lightweight institutional partnerships over enterprise SaaS.

---

# **Strategic Fit Evaluation**

## **A. NGO / Government Contracts**

**Fit Drivers**

* High mission alignment

* Low technical/ESG complexity

* Faster, project-based cycles

* Typical funding €5k–€50k

* Low entry barriers

**Verdict**

* Core revenue stream

* Strong match with capabilities and scope

---

## **B. Public-Sector Grants (EU, state, municipal)**

**Fit Drivers**

* Strong alignment with youth \+ sustainability priorities

* Compatible with challenge-based activation

* No enterprise SLAs

* Funds operations and development

**Constraints**

* Administrative load

* Limited predictability

**Verdict**

* High strategic fit

* Ideal early-stage funding and institutional legitimacy

---

## **C. University / Student-Union Licensing**

**Fit Drivers**

* Engagement budgets available

* Sustainability mandates

* Simple procurement

* Fast pilots

* Viable license range €5k–€20k

**Verdict**

* High fit

* Scalable, repeatable, short sales cycle

---

## **D. Corporate CSR Sponsorship (non-ESG)**

**Fit Drivers**

* CSR marketing budgets, not compliance

* Sponsorship of challenges and rewards

* Zero technical integration

**Constraints**

* Not recurring

* Requires sales outreach

* Needs basic impact metrics

**Verdict**

* Good secondary revenue

* Not core, but useful complement

---

## **E. Brand Reward Partnerships / Affiliates**

**Fit Drivers**

* Light implementation

* Works with student audience

* Scales with user numbers

**Constraints**

* Low early-stage revenue

* Dependent on partner incentives

* Margin limited without volume

**Verdict**

* Medium fit

* Suitable for later scaling, not early core income

---

## **F. B2B ESG / CSRD SaaS**

**Fit Issues**

* Heavy compliance requirements

* Audit-grade data

* Long enterprise cycles

* High legal and technical load

* Exceeds team capacity

**Verdict**

* Low fit

* Excluded from current strategy

---
