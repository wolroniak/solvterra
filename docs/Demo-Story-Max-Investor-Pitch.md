## Executive Summary

This document presents a compelling customer journey for **Max Mustermann**, our primary demo user representing the "Time-Pressed" persona (50% of our target audience). The story demonstrates how SolvTerra transforms micro-volunteering into an accessible, rewarding experience for busy students who want to help but lack time for traditional commitments.

**Key Metrics Demonstrated:**
- 12 challenges completed in 5 weeks
- 3.5 hours contributed (average 17.5 minutes per challenge)
- 280 XP earned, Level 2 "Helper" achieved
- 4 badges earned (showing progression)
- 5 NGOs supported

- 50+ kg vegetables sorted at Tafel

- Survey data for environmental research
- Book recommendations for education initiative
- First aid awareness spread

**For NGOs (Tafel example):**
- Traditional recruitment cost: ‚Ç¨50-100 per volunteer

### Why "Lebensmittel sortieren bei der Tafel" Is the Perfect Demo

1. **High emotional resonance:** Fighting food waste, feeding families
