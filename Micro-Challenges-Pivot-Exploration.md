## Validation Framework: Before You Commit

**Use the same rigorous process we used for CSRD:**

### 1. Customer Discovery (Do This First!)

**Talk to 10-15 potential customers:**
- 5 retail chains with multiple locations
- 5 market research companies
- 3-5 event companies

**Ask:**
- "How do you currently handle mystery shopping / fieldwork / event staffing?"
- "What frustrates you about current solutions?"
- "What would you pay for a better solution?"
- "What would make you switch from your current provider?"
- "How do you verify quality and prevent fraud?"

### 2. Competitive Analysis

**Research existing players:**
- Who are the top 5 competitors in Germany?
- What do they charge?
- What are their weaknesses?
- How would you differentiate?
- Why can't customers just do this themselves?

### 3. Unit Economics Validation

**Calculate:**
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) for both sides
- Lifetime Value (LTV) for retailers and students
- LTV:CAC ratio (should be > 3:1)
- Gross margin per transaction
- Monthly revenue needed to be sustainable

### 4. Technical Feasibility

**Validate:**
- Can you build the verification system? (Photo + GPS + AI validation)
- GDPR compliance for data collection?
- Fraud prevention mechanisms?
- Quality control at scale?

### 5. Team Fit Assessment

**Answer honestly:**
- Are we excited about this problem?
- Do we have domain knowledge or can we develop it quickly?
- Does this leverage our business + software engineering skills?
- Can we see ourselves working on this for 2-3 years?
